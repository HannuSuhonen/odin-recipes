Chicken with rice and salsa

Description

Very tasty chicken with rice and salsa! Goes very well with grilled chicken.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 black pepper
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup long-grain white rice
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup salsa

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, mis together the paprika, garlic powder, dried thyme, salt and black pepper
  2. Drizzle the olive oil over the chicken breasts and use your hands to massage the oil into the chicken
  3. Rub the spice mixture over thoroughly over both sides of the chicken breasts, making sure to coat them evenly
  4. Heat a large frying pan over medium high heat. Once hot, add the chicken breasts t the pan and cook for about 5-6 minutes on each side, or until golder brown and cooked through
  5. While the chicken is cooking, prepare the rice. Rinse the rice in a fine mesh strainer until the water runs clear. In a medium saucepan, bring the chicken broth and salt to a boil. Add the rinsed rice, stir well, and reduce the heat to low. Cover the saucepan and simmer for 18-20 minutes, or until the rice is tender and the liquid has been absorbed. You can also do this whole process in a rice cooker. Add the broth directly to the water and let it cook.
  6. Once the chicken and rice are done, serve the chicken with a side of rice and salsa.
  7. To serve, spoon some rice onto the plate, and place a cooked chicken breast on top of the rice. Spoon some salsa over the chicken and rice.
  8. Enjoy!
